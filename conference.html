2015

     The initial conference is being planned for Spring Semester 2015. Creating this conference provides students the opportunity to learn from leading professionals, develop networking relationships, and explore possible careers in the field. ISBC is composed of executive board members individually managing teams with specific responsibilities including but not limited to, alumni relations, finances, public relations and media. It is imperative to the ISBC team that the conference has a variety of speakers with prestigious backgrounds to give students a competitive edge when determining their niche in the sports business world.
