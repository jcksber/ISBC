Our Next Conference

February 27, 2015

Theme: Developing the process. Defining the industry.

Illinois Sports Business Conference (ISBC) was started by a group of students who saw an opportunity to fill the gap of knowledge and resources between the sports industry and the business world. The ISBC team works together throughout the year to put on an annual conference hosting elite leaders and professionals in the sports industry. Through various workshops, networking events, and an annual conference hosting leading professionals, ISBC fosters relationships and gives students a competitive edge as they seek their niche in the sports business world.
